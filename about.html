About this Project
  • Objective: Create a website to address the topic of Self-Care for Teachers
  • Solution: This website was created to address the topic of Self-Care for Teachers using the myriad of technological self-care tools and apps currently available on the Internet. Our target audience is any teacher or educator who has an interest in self-care, improving any aspect of their health, or may be curious to learn more. Use the navigation bar to explore the website, or click on any link on the page to read about a topic that interests you.
  • Year: Fall 2021
  • Client: Teachers
  • Role: Designer, Writer, Coder
  • Deliverable: Multi-Page Layout
  • Medium: Web Design
  • Software: Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, VS Code
