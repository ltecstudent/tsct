Teacher Self-Care in Technology

Self-Care for Teachers is an incredibly important topic of discussion in today’s reality of online teaching and remote learning. With the exponential growth of technology and technology-related tools such as videos, apps, and websites on the Internet, it may be hard for a teacher or educator to know where to begin to take care of their health. This website is meant to help this person by providing curated online resources of the best of Self-Care for Teachers, in a consolidated format.
