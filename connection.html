Connection

Harness the power of community and collectivity. Support and community are essential in the process of self-care, so making this a priority is a must. Finding ways to reach out and connect with family, friends, co-workers and others can be a challenge. We have gathered some suggestions below to make connecting with other teachers easier than ever.

Resources

Breathe for Change

This website describes themselves as “a movement enhancing the health and well-being of educators, students, and entire communities” and offers unique opportunities for teachers to train in areas such as: digital wellness, SEL, mindfulness practices, and yoga.

See website »

Teaching with Mental Health in Mind Facebook Group

A Facebook group that is “Committed to providing mental health resources and support to educators who come in contact with youth daily”.

see facebook »

Happy Teacher Revolution

A website that specifically targets the teacher community, Happy Teacher Revolution’s mission is to “support the mental health and wellness of teachers” by training teachers to become “Revolutionaries” around the world to gather pods of "Happy Teachers" in their local community.

See website »

Support Systems

Teachers Circle is a community where support and inclusion for educators is their mission. Via blogs and facilitated activities, teachers can come together in a safe place to share and grow.

See website »
